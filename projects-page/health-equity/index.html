Health Equity Data Challenge

Python Data Analysis Policy

Overview

A policy analysis project evaluating the effectiveness of Tennessee's opioid prescription policy by integrating healthcare and socioeconomic datasets to generate targeted, evidence-based recommendations for improving health outcomes in high-need communities.

Technical Details

Conducted policy impact assessment using interrupted time series analysis and regional segmentation to uncover disparities in opioid distribution trends across urban and rural counties in Eastern Tennessee. Results were presented through compelling data visualizations.

Results & Impact

Recognized as Runner-up at the Lucy Family Institute Health Equity Data Challenge, presenting to a panel of faculty judges and public health industry leaders.
